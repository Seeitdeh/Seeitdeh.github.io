Getting Started

OUTSIIDE is relatively new, so we've made onboarding as simple as possible.

1

Download the App

Use the App Store or Google Play links above to install OUTSIIDE on your device.

2

Create Your Profile

Sign up using your email address or phone number and set up your OUTSIIDE profile.

3

Verify & Explore

If signing up with email, please check your spam/junk folder for the verification email, then start exploring and enjoying OUTSIIDE.
